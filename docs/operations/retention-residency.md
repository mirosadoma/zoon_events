# Retention and residency

Owner: Privacy Operations  
Last reviewed: 2026-07-03

Tenant policy identifies an approved region and retention profile. Audit evidence defaults
to 365 days; policy may select 30–3650 days subject to legal hold. Exports expire within
5–1440 minutes and their private files are deleted while request/download evidence remains.
Cross-border movement requires a separately approved adapter/readiness record.
